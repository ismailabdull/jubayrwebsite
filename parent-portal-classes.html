Class Levels

📚

Beginner (Hingaad)

Focus: Learning how to read Arabic (hingaad) in order to recite the Qur'an properly.

Teachers:

Macalin Xamdi, Macalin Mahdi, Macalin Zahra, Macalin Naima, Macalin Nuun, Macalin Ikhlas, Macalin Faisa, Macalin Shahnas, Macalin Amira, Macalin Rahma, Macalin Hani

🕌

Intermediate

Focus: Qur'an reading and memorization.

Teachers:

Macalin Ismail, Macalin Anas, Macalin Abdullahi Ali, Macalin Abdullahi Abdi, Macalin Ibrahim, Macalin Siham, Macalin Maymuuna

🌟

Machad (Advanced)

Focus: Older students learning Islamic studies alongside Qur'an.

Teachers:

Macalin Hamza and Macalin Abdirahman Dhaqane
